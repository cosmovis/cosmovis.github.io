CosmoVis: Culture, Cosmos & Creation

An interdisciplinary association exploring the universe through science, art, education, and imagination.

Our Projects

Cosmovisions

Linking astronomy with ancestral and cultural interpretations of the universe through artistic and educational formats.

Cosmoneo

An astronomy-inspired board game where players evolve cosmic elements, form stars, and explore the birth of galaxies.

Bake Your Own Universe

A delicious workshop that teaches cosmology through cooking — from dark matter to cosmic inflation in your frying pan.

About CosmoVis

Founded by Sofia Gallego, CosmoVis is a Paris-based association dedicated to interdisciplinary exploration and outreach at the crossroads of astrophysics, culture, and creativity. We organize immersive events, design educational tools, and connect people to the universe in new, poetic ways.
